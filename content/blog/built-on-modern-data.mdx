Most software you can buy for housing was built a decade ago, on top of a database that was built a decade before that. That shows up in the day-to-day: data trapped in PDFs, exports that never quite line up, integrations that are spreadsheets in disguise, and reports that take a week to produce because the underlying records can't be trusted.

The foundation matters more than any single feature. So we started there.

## A clean data model

Stairpay's data model treats every concept in Shared Ownership as a first-class object: leases, plots, residents, transactions, charges, valuations, documents, cases. Each one has a defined shape, a clear lifecycle, and a stable identifier. Nothing important lives in a free-text field.

That means data is queryable instead of guessable. Reports are produced from the underlying records, not reconciled across spreadsheets. And when the regulator asks a question, the answer is already structured — not a PDF dump.

## Real APIs, not exports

Every object in Stairpay has an API. That's how we connect to mortgage providers, valuation networks, document signing, payment rails, and the back-office systems Housing Associations already run. Not via flat-file imports overnight, but via real-time, authenticated, audited calls.

Integrations stop being a project and start being a setting. New partners can be onboarded in weeks rather than quarters.

## Built to scale

The stack underneath Stairpay was chosen to grow. Modern infrastructure, sensible boundaries, observability everywhere. We can run one Housing Association with a hundred plots or a national provider with tens of thousands — on the same platform, with the same SLAs.

## The foundation for what's next

Housing data isn't just for reports. Done properly, it becomes housing intelligence — patterns across portfolios, predictive signals on staircasing and resales, automated handling of routine work. That future is only possible on top of a foundation that's clean, connected, and computable.

That's the foundation we've built. It's why Housing Associations that join Stairpay don't just digitise their existing workflows — they unlock what comes next.
